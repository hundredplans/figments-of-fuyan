The Sugori may be pirates and gatherers but they do have a military system, There is a leader of some sorts, called the
Mala, then the Giras and the Joloise. 

The Mala, which is currently Vanessa Haliness, controls the General Army, in charge of war, affairs and sometimes
policing. The Mala does not have a pirate crew. Malas in the past had differing views often, but generally
they have upmost respect thrown at them and they all think of their people first.

The Giras are the 4 most powerful pirate captains from the Sugori ilses, they are a big contributer to the economy,
have huge reputation and popularity and help deal with issues that arise around, they seperately do cover different
areas but not enough to have their own specific roles, the Giras are appointed by Mala but historically no Mala has 
ever changed the Giras themselves, with one exception. 
The current 4 Giras are: Gus, Morgan V. Pikefist, Yves von Eric and Gorgana Wretchirl. Giras are really important
for balance, they also are a sole reason for many rookies to enter pirating as these kids grow up to want to be with 
these Giras. They all specialise in a seperate technique of fighting so that they can counter any opposition, very 
rarely do Giras pass away during service but if they do, national holidays get dished out and the whole nation 
mourns.

Then in the next tier stand the Joloise, there are several dozens of these. The Joloise splits into 2 seperate groups the 
Lords and the Warriors. The Lords are a policing regiment, these pirate groups are assigned their specific province/island 
to protect and police. Meanwhile the Warriors are the nations most prolific explorers, and combatants. The Joloise still
have a lot of reputation just not as much as the Giras or Vanessa.
At the top sits the Saint Joloise, who currently is Vector Barbossa, he keeps track of both Joloise groups so that they
do their job correctly. Vector Barbossa is arguably as powerful/important as the Gira, but stands on a
lower step. Under the Saint Joloise stands King Lord Ardina Lockhart and King Warrior Nidon the Restless, which chief
over the 2 respective Joloise groups. They do not neccesarily take directions from Vector Barbossa but they follow any
instructions and more thoroughly govern the 2 groups, to lighten the job for Vector. The Saint Joloise is picked by
Vanessa and the Saint also picks his two Kings, the Giras and Mala can veto any Kings from stepping into power but they 
have yet to do so.

Left are the Minors, which are the majority of Pirate captains in the system, these guys run on the same level and they 
do typical pirate behaviors, there is a scale of reputation, some pirates are only known locally, others are known over
larger areas but no matter what, anyone who assembles a crew will begin as a Minor Pirate Captain unless they get 
promoted by their province or island to being a Joloise.

Lastly, there is the General Army, these soldiers are trained like pirates but do not have a captain, they are almost
like a navy. The General Army is led by Vanessa, she has the Second in Command, Mina Elroark and then there are a lot of
Army Commanders, which can be promoted by Mina or Vanessa. This General Army is only used for warfare or if an 
emmergency is in place, otherwise they only train in wait for something like so to happen.
