Jules Maximus is the President of Zengef who runs the Prasedi (government). He is 53, tall but slim with short greying hair and blue eyes. Jules was elected 8 years ago, the end of his presidency is coming up as The President is democratically elected exactly every decade from among the political parties of Zengef. Jules is of the Combat-Coalition, similar to his predecessor, due to the unrest caused by Lafef's Coup, he is also a non mage due to laws set in place following the coup.
