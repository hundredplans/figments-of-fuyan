Zengefian mages that are considered the best in their respective element, when they take the title they disband their old name and take the name of their element. They are expected to teach students to hopefully raise a new generation, more powerful than the last, not all Great Masters take this duty seriously.

Zoyana Fireblood: Wields "Pinprick" and a white glove, also called the "Dancing Flame"
Gofa Waterblood: Known as the "Greatest Master", unofficial head of the Great Master's
Rakinji Windblood: Blind Unim nomad, named "Duststorm" for his iconic attack
Draco Thunderblood: "Dragon of Zengef" fights using his fists and bolts of lightning.
Leon Earthblood: Golem maker, uses lifemagic to animate his golems
