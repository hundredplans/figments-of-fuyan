The Great Masters are an independent body of five capable mages, each considered the peak in one of the five elements. They are elected by an unanimous vote of the other Great Masters, including the departing one if he lives. Once raised to the title a Great Master will symbolically leave their old life behind, exchanging their last name for that of their element. The Great Masters, in order of seniority are: Draco Thunderblood, Gofa Waterblood, Rakinji Windblood, Leon Earthblood and Zoyana Fireblood.

The Great Masters have been part of Zengefian culture as long as the country.
