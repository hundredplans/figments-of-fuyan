The Mage Army of Zengef Structure

General - Responsible for strategy
	Vice General - Advisor to the general and often gives orders in place of the General
		Commanders (8) - Command roughly 4,000 men each (2 for each non thunder element)
			Captains (4-6) - Amount based on commander, in charge of 600-1000 men
				Chiefs (8-12) - In charge of platoons of up to 100 men
					Officers (6-15) - In charge of typically a squad (5-15 men), some are divided by elements, some are multipurpose

		The Nobles (30-60) - Called the Nobles as these are typically already formed squads donated by a Magelord, they are much 												more specialised and have very diverse amounts of mages capable of a lot, the leaders of these squads 												are called Noble Officers (as they typically are). If a Magelord wants to train his son he will send him in 												command of a Noble so the quality of these squads vary drastically. Only the Vice General and General 												can officially command a Noble but Commanders typically have a lot of power over them regardless.

Some Officers command how the squad shoots, pointing it in a direction rather than having individual members shoot wily-nily
