The Rumble Knight "Swordbolt" was the youngest person to become a Commander, almost a decade ago when he was twenty-one. He is stationed in High Enkefen and is the only regiment within Enkefen. He commands seven thousand men, which most often patrol the border of the duchy and prevent banditry. He is helped by his Assistant General, Dives Loisotto. Dives is not much of a fighter, only learning Advanced Watermagic at the National Academy, however his strategical genius is unparalleled within his generation. He graduated top of the class in War Strategy at the National Academy. This has done irreperable damage to his view of himself, and the previously haughty, arrogant Dives has become almost insufferable.

"Earthbear", "Fat" or "Feral" Klaus is a Commander of the Tenos Regiment alongside Pistol Preston. He is of noble Stoneland origin, plain by his girth, height, alcoholism and loud personality. He abandoned his parents when he decided to pursue Earthmagic at the age of twenty-two, by travelling to Zengef as a merchant's guard. Settling in High Enkefen, he fought in Eva's Ring to pay the bills. Klaus was an exceptionally strong Earthmage, fighting by wrapping his body in Earthmagic and creating massive claws of earth, he became the undefeated Champion for two years in a row. He was eventually defeated by the Rumble Knight, who soon after left the Ring. Klaus had a midlife crisis, he left the ring and attempted to become a student of Leon Earthblood who rejected him. Dejected, he once again travelled as a merchant guard, engaging in excessive drinking. As a proud person, he was unsatisfied with his lack of achievement and so joined the Tenos Regiment, quickly rising up the ranks. As a Commander, he fell in love with the other Commander alongside him, Angela Darspes, a Windmage. Klaus gave up drinking for her, and was esctatic during this time of his life. As happens to most Commanders, Angela died in battle to an enemy marksman. Klaus was devastated, and returned to his drinking habits. When Pistol Preston replaced Angela, Klaus treated him poorly. Overtime he warmed up to him, and now they are good friends.

Pistol Preston, self-styled the "Dapper Gunslinger" is a Commander of the Tenos Regiment alongside Fat Klaus. He is a short man, always wearing a tophat which only highlights his small size. Of Dalanet origin, Preston began in a well-off family of gun smugglers. He would tinker with the guns at his parent's home while they were waiting to be smuggled, finding their mechanisms fascinating. Eventually his parent's boss, Felix Lesli-Shine of the Tophats, blew his parent's head off when they refused to take on extra risk. Preston managed to escape due to the kindness of a stranger, Jonas, who adopted him as his own. Thus began Preston's quest for vengeance, where he learnt how to handle his gun aswell as perfected his craftsmanship. After half a decade Preston snuck into the Tophat's HQ and killed Felix the same way the mafia boss killed his parents, with a shotgun blast to the face. Preston took the white tophat, now riddled with bullets, and went back to Jonas. His stepfather could not accept housing a killer, and so in words of rage told him to get out of his house and out of Dalanet. Dejected and alone, Preston followed his words and left for Zengef whereupon he found the Mage Army of Zengef. He discovered an abnormal talent with all the elements, likely via his early exposure and began using five different guns, one for each element.

Icaff Vifon, known as "Rainmaker" is the oldest current living Commander of the tumultous Pulfes Regiment. Icaff was born into the Vifon family, but despite his noble upbringing his life was not easy. Many believed Icaff slow when he was in his younger years. He just liked taking time to consider things. The one person who saw his intelligence and potential, Kalissa Fireblood, died in front of his eyes. Ever since then Icaff has pondered why he survived. He dedicated his life to the Mage Army of Zengef since then and honed his Watermagic to a deadliness that sees him alive after almost two decades. Rainmaker wears a pointed hat and uses a long white staff to aim his attacks, both gifts from Kalissa that hold no practical utility. His title is due to his ability to form clouds or fill them with water until bursting. It's an immeasurable advantage against enemy armies that saw him redeployed to Pulfes from Enkefen when the Stoneland skirmishes began.
