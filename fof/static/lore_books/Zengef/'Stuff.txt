	Tenos - Capital city is Tenos agriciultural greenland. Held by Duchess Tenos (Catherine), 2nd most influential 
		City of Tenos - Pretty big city, not much going on

	Tafos - Capital city is Mercaju. Held by Duke Cupid, most influential, non-mage strong contender for Head of State.
		Aronka - A tunnel city and Lafef's birthplace, quite poor as Duke Cupid prefers to focus on Mercaju
		Mercaju - The trade center of Zengef

	Pulfes - Doesn't have an official capital city, but Castellum Fort is well-known. Held by Duke Bellam, 5th most influential
		Castellum Fort - Defends the East Coast from the Stoneland and the HQ of the Mage Army of Zengef
		Catarac Waterfalls - Tourist attraction, Tafos has tried to own this land for centuries
		Clifftown - The only town that cultivates Thundermages in Zengef, Draco's and Kyon's Thunderbloods birthplace

	Carielam - Capital city is Undatif, responsible for sea-trade and trade with Mercaju. Held by Duke Flumen. 4th most influential
		Undatif - Port city brings in a lot of foreigners and travellers and so is the most disliked city by Enkefians
		Gofalin - Previously named Seatown renamed in honor of the "Greatest Master" to Gofalin, where Gofa Waterblood grew up
