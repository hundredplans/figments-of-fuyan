Hufo Perkot is the General of the Mage Army of Zengef, more commonly referred to simply as The General. He is 47, a powerful Firemage and once a student of the late Kallisa Fireblood. He turned down succeeding Kallisa after her death in Lafef's Coup when he was offered the position 22 years ago (2974PD) as the General of the newly formed Mage Army of Zengef. Hufo is 211cm tall, extremely tall for a Zengefian, and has a perpetually taut face on which he always wears sunglasses to protect his eyes. He owns a pet pheonix, which he named Max that he rides and fights on.
