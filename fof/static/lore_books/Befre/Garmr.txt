

                          Garmr

 
 In the veil of snow where secrets sleep,
 A spectre of the forest's deep.
 A creature vast, in silence weaves,
 Through moonlit pines, where bushes breathe.

 Eyes unseen in the cloak of night,
 A phantom king with fur of white.
 Whispers of cold in the creepy air,
 A sound it takes, beyond despair
