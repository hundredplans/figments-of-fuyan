Aura is an invisible bubble around you, typically starting at your heart. It prevents the infusion of anyone within, unless you subconciously or conciously allow them to Infuse. Aura's also naturally destroy any infused magic that enter it, what this practically means is another mage will lose control of what they infuse when it enters your Aura. A full grown average adult's aura is about the size of a regular man's wingspan. Most mages are able to detect other's Auras, as after enough practice with magic you get a tingling sensation when within an Aura. If a mage wishes to be covert, they can restrict their Aura to that of a regular man. You can test how far inside another's Aura you are by experimenting with how well you can Infuse.

Aura's are believed to live in the God Realm, where mana resides, which is why they are invisible. They are said to have two properties that can increase, their size and resistance. An Aura's size if how large the soul is, however a large Aura does not imply a strong one. Resistance is how much Infusion the Aura resists, it's inversely proportional to the distance. Strength is the term used to roughly describe the size and resistance of a given Aura, as though they are not necessarily related it is often that with size comes resistance. The strength of your Aura has many factors that determine it: The strength of your parent's Aura, time training with magic, pursuing Fuyan and training your soul. Monsters, defined as animals which can use magic typically with strong Auras, seem to mostly possess a similar strength of Aura among all their kin.

Due to the Aura's relation to the soul, a dying person's Aura will wane significantly.
