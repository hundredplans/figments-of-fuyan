The magic of Elvus is powered by raw mana and the Blessings of Gods. The Blessing of Creation was bestowed by the First Man/Creator, an element to master was given to each of the disciples that matched their predisposition. These disciples passed their blessings down by blood, and in this day all the mages of the world are descendants of the disciples. One element of the blessings, that of Life, which was given to Nolak, is not known to the wider world. Instead it is hidden in the island of Nolaka as the disciple knew the power of his magic, and decided to hide it from the evils of men. The other 5 elements, that of Fire, Water, Earth, Wind and Thunder (electricity) are known to exist by most mages, particularly Zengefians. What most mages do not know, is that if they can use one element, they are thereotically capable of all 5. The First Man purposefully hid this knowledge by giving an element to master to each disciple, as he was scared of the feats men could accomplish with the gift of all 5 elements combined.

The Blessing of Sacrifice is given by the Blood God, K'haal. The practicioners of this magic are known as Bloodmages, they are typically not respected as proper magicians, especially by Zengefians. The Blessing of Sacrifice requires a sacrifice to use, but in return is capable of many feats unachievable by mages, or as the Bloodmages call them, Creation Mages. Since sacrifice is not always possible, K'haal himself acts as a storage for the power. He requires grand sacrifices, often massacres of villages or the blood of a battlefield to sustain his power, he grants this power to his followers when they need it.
