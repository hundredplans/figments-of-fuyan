The current Stone Emperor, Galkin Kamen, has ruled the Stoneland and it's many colonies for 23 years. His title in the Stoneland is Galkin and he sits on the Silverstone. The Galkin is typically chosen from one of the three factions of the Stoneland.

- The Sterkz, also known as the Expansion Faction, are ruled by Zvir Blackeyes. Medved was a prolific member of this faction and was sent away by Kamen to sit on the Rustic Throne. It is the second strongest faction politically.

- The Izgroi, the Foreigner Faction, are ruled by Saint Jankali, a Shreevian. They are formed of the previous kings and high-ranking nobles of vassal nations who swore allegiance to the Stone Emperor and moved to Stoneland. They are a very splintered faction with several important members fighting for power, each trying to get the best for their own country. It is the weakest faction politically and has been ever since Galkin's Stranet's death.

- The Derzgat, the Loyalist Faction, are ruled by Duke Karasel. Kamen was the previous head of this faction. The Derzgat support defending their current lands instead of expanding, believing expanding too far will lead to the Stone Empire's fall. It has been the strongest faction politically ever since Kamen's crowning.
