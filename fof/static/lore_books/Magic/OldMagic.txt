The magic of Elvus is powered by raw (ambient) mana and blessings of gods. A blessing is bestowed upon a person's soul, and all descendants of that person and they gain the ability to transform raw mana into a usable form. The Blessing of Creation, or what is commonly thought of as regular magic in this world, was bestowed by the First Man/Creator. It provides the user the ability to transform raw mana into Pure Magic. Water, Wind, Electricity, Fire and Earth are the most common manifestations of Pure Magic and therefore nicknamed the elements, there are many practicioners who do not know Pure Magic can achieve more than these elements. With enough training, a magic user can manifest the Pure Magic into anything, some can even regenerate body parts. The only fuel a magic user requires is raw mana, which is more than plentiful for any spellcasting in most areas of the world.

Another blessing is that of the Blood God, Khaal. It is the Blessing of Sacrifice, or more commonly referred to as Blood Magic. Those that practice this magic can only use it through sacrifice, the sacrifice is transformed into raw mana then blood magic by the user. Khaal also serves as a storage for sacrifice. Khaal stores the raw mana gained from sacrifice in himself, his followers use the raw mana within him to cast blood magic themself.
