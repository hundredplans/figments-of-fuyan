The Varoma Mountains have three traversable paths, Boulder's way leads to the aptly named Boulder, a massive darkstone Varoma fortress resembling a boulder. This path is primarily controlled by the Ka'luta as it leads into their territority. The Old Road now lays abandoned, as any traveller is attacked by the supersized critters that resurface from the caves or the snow monsters found on the mountain peaks, it is used only by the most desperate adventurers. Finally, the Stone Road, leads directly to the Stoneland the mainland of the Stone Empire. Ka'luta often raid the section that is within their territory and so the Bulwark's army, the Border Brothers extend their protection to the Stoneroad's pathway, often sending patrols to protect delievery of important goods from the Stoneland, or a merchant that has paid for their protection.
