Silverstone is a highly resistant but lightweight material used primarily by the Stone Empire to arm their soldiers. It's origins are unknown although it is widely believed they are found in caves under mountains, a dangerous place for any but the most prepared. The Silver Army of Varoma and other Stone Empire colonies are provided a certain amount of silverstone to do with as they please, Varoma use them to create silverstone shields for the regular soldiers and full armor for officers or higher. It is illegal to trade overseas without government permission, this rule is strictly enforced as the Stone Empire fears it will lose it's military advantage if other nations have enough to arm their armies.
