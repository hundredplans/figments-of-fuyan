================Hama Cik==============

The Hama Cik is believed to be an ancient race of humans that were once on this earth
or are going to be on this earth. This is because they are believed to be explorers or 
researchers of Space and Time. Their only remainings found so far were an island with 
monuments and an ancient dialect supposedly theirs. There is not much to none 
information on the Hama Cik so all of these were common rumours/guesses to who they 
were, the name Hama Cik didn't even have any correlation to anything it was a name 
given by the people who merely found the island. The Name means (Mysterious Ones) in
______ese, the island was found in the year _____ by the _____.

The most popular theory is that the Hama Cik either fled from something or simply were 
curious to space time, they succeeded and either co-exist(ed) in this timeline or a 
parralel timeline. Due to the fact they already were in the past they are believed to 
have travelled to the Future and will one day be hopefully spotted, and hopefully not 
murdered. Despite the wars or conflicts, the Powers have indirectly agreed to try 
conserve any spottings of Hama Cik to keep them alive when they must appear even if 
there was no agreement to do so.
