Long ago the First Man created Elvus, our home. He was loved by all and helped civilizations rise. At one point, he announced his leave without reason, he told his most trusted followers to keep the world stable and to take the care of it. In part of his closing remarks, he mentions "Once you achieve Fuyan, I will invite you to my doorstep". For many centuries after, the people followed his will and remained encouraged by his teachings but over time unity was lost. Those now in power seek to find Fuyan, each with a completely different interpretation of its meaning.
